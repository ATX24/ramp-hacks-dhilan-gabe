# Humanizer pass: Announcing TinyFable

## Voice target

Research-lab release note written by the people who built the system. Direct, technically specific, and willing to describe limits without turning every paragraph into a disclaimer.

## Patterns removed

- Generic claims about making distillation easy.
- The old eight-example, twelve-step training story, which belonged to a different system design.
- Artificial symmetry and repeated three-item slogans.
- Vague praise such as robust, seamless, novel, and powerful.
- Claims that QLoRA itself makes a model smaller.
- Placeholder benchmark numbers and broad production claims.
- Em dashes and en dashes.

## Final editorial choices

- Open with the practical trace-reuse problem.
- Name both primary finance tasks early.
- State the successful release status, then define its narrow meaning.
- Explain losing proof states as product outcomes.
- Show the actual three-line SDK path.
- Keep the satire in one paragraph instead of sprinkling jokes through the technical explanation.
