# 30-second pitch

> Companies already have traces and accounting workflows, but distillation
> products either make them write a training system or blindly fine-tune.
> Distillery gives you one high-level path: curate the traces, synthesize only
> the labels you are missing, train TinyFable as a smaller portable finance
> generalist, and prove whether it wins. TinyFable handles transaction review
> and variance analysis. Then we compare base, teacher, sequence KD, logit KD,
> rules, and a cheap API with confidence intervals and break-even sensitivity.
> If a baseline is better, Distillery says do not distill. Smaller models.
> Proven economics.
